Exercise 3: Monte Carlo Methods

A Monte Carlo method approximates the area of a region by taking a large sample of uniformly distributed points over an area that covers that region and evaluating the proportion of points that fall inside it.

Circle Area Approximation

import numpy as np
from matplotlib import pyplot as plt

x = np.linspace(-1, 1, 100)
y1 = (1 - x**2)**0.5
y2 = -(1 - x**2)**0.5

plt.plot(x, y1, color='white')
plt.plot(x, y2, color='white')
ax = plt.gca()
ax.axis('equal')
ax.set_facecolor('black')
ax.fill_between(x, y1, y2, facecolor='white')
plt.show()
